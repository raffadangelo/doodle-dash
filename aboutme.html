Who am I?

Hi! My name is Raffaele D'Angelo, I'm 16 years old and I live in Italy with my family. I'm a studious guy and I love drawing! I created this website to share my passion for drawing superheroes, anime and manga with the whole world.

In the various sections of the site you will find my gallery, my store where you can buy my drawings! Keep following me for new creations and artistic adventures!

I started drawing when I was a child, and over the years, I have developed my skills through constant practice and learning. My favorite characters to draw are superheroes because they inspire me to create stories of bravery and heroism.

Besides drawing, I enjoy watching anime and reading manga, which fuel my creativity and provide endless inspiration for my art.

My dream is to become a professional artist and to see my characters come to life in comics and animations.
